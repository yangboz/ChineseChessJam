MiniMax — class, package com.lookbackon.AI.searching
MiniMax.as class. The idea here is that both players will try all possible moves in their position and then choose, respectively, the one which makes the value of the position as high as possible (the white side) or as low as possible (black). I have called one color 'WHITE', this is the side which tries to maximize the value, and the other side tries to minimize the value.
